OutFlankBall Rules

Rules

    1.Field Sizes
  1. 1V1(SOLO): 2x4M.
  2. 2V2(DUO): 4x6M.
  3. 3V3(TRIO): 4x8M.
  4. 5V5(TEAM): 6x10M.

    2.Gameplay

    Match Starting

  • The teams will choose a coin sides
  • The judge throws a coin, wich side is up, that's team starting round.
  • player with ball is staying with the back to the line.
  • Other players stay where they want.
  • After whistle or clap, game is starting.

      • Match Playing

  • Players need to touch a opponent team zone with ball.
  • Players don't allowed a throw ball to zone, but allowed to pass to other players (If Solo, player can throw ball in air).
  • Players don't allowed to drown other players.
  • If opponent fall by your drowning, round stops, and starting penalty (Ball is giving to injured teammate team.)
  • Players not alowed to go out of field.
  • If any team don't play with rules, penalty.

      • Round Ending

  • If any player touch opponent score area, he's team got 1 point.
  • If ball was thrown, it's not score.

      • Match Winners

  • Match have 21 round. (Penalty's not round)
  • Wins that team what in 21 round get more point than other team.
  • Prize is official Diploma and medal (Medal is not necessarily)
